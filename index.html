Account Deletion Request

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

This page explains how users of the Connect NRI – Jalandhar District Community Initiative Application can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

Important: This is not an official Government of India or Punjab Government application. It is an independent district-level community initiative.

1. Requesting Account Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account by contacting our support team.

Steps to Request Deletion

  • Send an email from your registered email address.
  • Email address: sevapani08@gmail.com
  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request – Connect NRI
  • Include your registered email address or username in the message.

2. What Happens After Your Request

  • Your account will be permanently deleted.
  • Your profile information will be removed.
  • Your posts, comments, and uploaded content will be deleted.
  • Your personal information associated with the account will be erased from our systems.

3. Data Retention

Certain limited technical logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance purposes. After this period, the data is automatically deleted.

4. Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 5–7 business days after verification.

5. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding account deletion or your data, you can contact us:

Email: sevapani08@gmail.com
Response Time: Within 7 business days

By submitting an account deletion request, you confirm that you are the owner of the account associated with the request.
